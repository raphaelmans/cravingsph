# US-ADMIN-003: Manage Restaurant Records

**As a** platform admin,
**I want** to edit, deactivate, or feature restaurant records,
**So that** I can maintain platform quality and merchandising.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** a restaurant record exists, **When** I update, deactivate, or feature it through admin controls, **Then** the restaurant record reflects that change.
- **Given** a restaurant is deactivated, **When** customers try to discover or order from it, **Then** the restaurant is no longer available as an active listing.
