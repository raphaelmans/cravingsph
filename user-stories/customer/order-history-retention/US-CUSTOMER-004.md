# US-CUSTOMER-004: Read Ratings And Reviews

**As a** customer,
**I want** to see ratings and reviews on restaurant pages,
**So that** I can make informed choices before ordering.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** a restaurant has published reviews, **When** I open its page, **Then** I can see rating aggregates and review content.
- **Given** a restaurant has no reviews yet, **When** I open its page, **Then** I see a no-reviews state instead of misleading summary data.
