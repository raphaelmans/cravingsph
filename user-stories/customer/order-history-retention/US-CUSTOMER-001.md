# US-CUSTOMER-001: View Past Orders

**As a** customer,
**I want** to view my past orders when I have an account,
**So that** I can look back at previous purchases.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I am signed in and have completed orders, **When** I open order history, **Then** I can see my prior orders with status and summary details.
- **Given** I have no prior orders, **When** I view order history, **Then** I see an empty state instead of an error.
