# US-CUSTOMER-005: Receive Promotional Notifications

**As a** customer,
**I want** to receive promotional notifications from restaurants I have ordered from,
**So that** I can hear about deals and new items.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I have opted in to marketing notifications, **When** a restaurant I have ordered from sends a promotion, **Then** I may receive that promotional notification.
- **Given** I opt out of promotional notifications, **When** new promotions are sent, **Then** they are not delivered to me while transactional notifications remain unaffected.
