# US-CUSTOMER-003: Rate And Review A Restaurant

**As a** customer,
**I want** to rate and review a restaurant after a completed order,
**So that** I can share my experience.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** an order is completed, **When** I submit a rating and review, **Then** the review is linked to that restaurant and my account.
- **Given** an order is not yet completed, **When** I attempt to review it, **Then** the review action is unavailable or blocked until completion.
