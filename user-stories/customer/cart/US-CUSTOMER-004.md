# US-CUSTOMER-004: Review Itemized Cart Totals

**As a** customer,
**I want** to see an itemized breakdown with totals,
**So that** I know exactly what I am paying for.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** my cart has one or more items, **When** I open the cart, **Then** each line shows quantity, selected options, unit pricing, and line total.
- **Given** modifiers add extra cost, **When** totals are calculated, **Then** those modifier amounts are included in the line total and overall cart total.
