# US-CUSTOMER-006: See Live Price Updates

**As a** customer,
**I want** to see the total update as I customize an item,
**So that** I know the cost before adding it to cart.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I change the variant, modifiers, or quantity, **When** the customization state updates, **Then** the displayed total recalculates immediately.
- **Given** I clear paid modifiers or reduce quantity, **When** the price recalculates, **Then** the total decreases to match the remaining selections.
