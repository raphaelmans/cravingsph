# US-CUSTOMER-005: Use Scan QR CTA From Home

**As a** customer,
**I want** a clear scan QR call to action on the home page,
**So that** I can quickly jump to in-store menu browsing.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I land on the home page, **When** I look at the primary actions, **Then** there is a visible Scan QR option.
- **Given** I choose the Scan QR action, **When** that flow opens, **Then** I am taken to the QR scanning or QR-entry experience instead of a generic browse page.
