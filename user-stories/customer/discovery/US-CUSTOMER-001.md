# US-CUSTOMER-001: Browse Nearby And Featured Restaurants

**As a** customer,
**I want** to see nearby or featured restaurants on the home page,
**So that** I can discover places to eat without already knowing a name.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** discovery data is available, **When** I open the home page, **Then** I see featured and nearby restaurant sections with current listing data.
- **Given** location access is unavailable, **When** the home page loads, **Then** featured restaurants still render and nearby results degrade gracefully.
