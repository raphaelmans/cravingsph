# US-CUSTOMER-006: Save Restaurants For Later

**As a** customer,
**I want** to bookmark restaurants for later,
**So that** I can quickly return to favorites.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I am signed in, **When** I bookmark or unbookmark a restaurant, **Then** that favorite state is saved to my account.
- **Given** I am not signed in, **When** I try to save a restaurant, **Then** the app prompts me to sign in or create an account before saving favorites.
