# US-CUSTOMER-005: Add Special Instructions

**As a** customer,
**I want** to include special instructions with my order,
**So that** the restaurant can accommodate reasonable preferences.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I am reviewing checkout, **When** I enter special instructions, **Then** they are saved on the order and visible to restaurant staff.
- **Given** I leave instructions blank, **When** I submit the order, **Then** checkout still succeeds without requiring that field.
