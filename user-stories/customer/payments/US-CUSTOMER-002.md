# US-CUSTOMER-002: Copy Payment Details

**As a** customer,
**I want** to copy payment account numbers from checkout,
**So that** I can transfer money quickly and accurately.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** payment details are visible, **When** I tap the copy action for an account number, **Then** that value is copied to my clipboard.
- **Given** copy succeeds, **When** the action completes, **Then** I receive immediate feedback that the payment detail was copied.
