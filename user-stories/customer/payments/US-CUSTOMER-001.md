# US-CUSTOMER-001: View Accepted Payment Methods

**As a** customer,
**I want** to see the restaurant's accepted payment methods and account details,
**So that** I know where to send payment.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** a branch has configured payment methods, **When** I reach the payment step, **Then** I can see each enabled method and its account details.
- **Given** a branch marks one payment method as default, **When** the list is shown, **Then** that preferred method is presented first or clearly highlighted.
