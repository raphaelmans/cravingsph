# US-OWNER-005: Mark Dine-In Orders Paid In Cash

**As a** restaurant owner,
**I want** a shortcut to mark dine-in orders as paid in cash,
**So that** I can quickly confirm counter payments.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** a dine-in order is payable in cash, **When** I mark it as Paid (Cash), **Then** the order is recorded as paid without digital proof submission.
- **Given** the order is not eligible for cash confirmation, **When** I view the payment controls, **Then** the Paid (Cash) shortcut is unavailable.
