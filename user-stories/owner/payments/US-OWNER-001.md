# US-OWNER-001: Configure Accepted Payment Methods

**As a** restaurant owner,
**I want** to configure accepted payment methods for my organization,
**So that** customers know where and how to pay.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I am managing payments, **When** I add a GCash, Maya, or bank payment method with required account details, **Then** it becomes available to eligible customer checkouts.
- **Given** required account details are incomplete, **When** I save the payment method, **Then** the configuration is rejected until the missing details are supplied.
