# US-OWNER-005: Revoke Team Member Access

**As a** restaurant owner,
**I want** to revoke team member access at any time,
**So that** I maintain control over who can manage my restaurant.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** a team member currently has access, **When** I revoke that access, **Then** they can no longer use the restaurant management features for that restaurant.
- **Given** access has been revoked, **When** I review the team list, **Then** the revoked member no longer appears as an active operator.
