# US-OWNER-002: Assign Roles And Permissions

**As a** restaurant owner,
**I want** to assign manager or viewer roles with granular permissions,
**So that** staff only access what they need.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** a team member is invited or active, **When** I assign a role and permission set, **Then** those access rules are stored for that team member.
- **Given** I update a team member's role, **When** the change is saved, **Then** subsequent access checks use the updated permission set.
