# US-OWNER-006: See Verification Status

**As a** restaurant owner,
**I want** to see whether my verification is pending, approved, or rejected,
**So that** I know whether I can go live or need to take action.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** my restaurant has a verification status, **When** I view onboarding or settings, **Then** the current status is shown as pending, approved, or rejected.
- **Given** verification is rejected, **When** I view the status, **Then** I can tell that the restaurant is not yet live and needs follow-up action.
