# US-OWNER-003: Add Restaurant Profile Details

**As a** restaurant owner,
**I want** to enter my restaurant's basic information,
**So that** customers can find and trust my listing.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I am on the restaurant information step, **When** I submit the restaurant name, description, address, location, contact details, and photos, **Then** the restaurant record is saved with those fields.
- **Given** required restaurant profile fields are missing, **When** I try to continue, **Then** the wizard shows validation errors and keeps me on the same step.
