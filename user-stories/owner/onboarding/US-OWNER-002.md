# US-OWNER-002: Complete Guided Setup Wizard

**As a** restaurant owner,
**I want** a setup wizard that walks me through creating my organization, restaurant, branch, and menu,
**So that** I can go live step by step.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I am a new owner without setup progress, **When** I open onboarding, **Then** I can move through the configured setup steps in order.
- **Given** I complete a step, **When** I continue or return later, **Then** my progress is preserved and previously completed steps remain marked complete.
