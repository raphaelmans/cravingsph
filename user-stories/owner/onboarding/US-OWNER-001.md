# US-OWNER-001: Register As Restaurant Owner

**As a** restaurant owner,
**I want** to register and set my portal preference to owner,
**So that** I am routed into the restaurant management experience.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** I am creating a new account, **When** I choose the owner portal preference during registration, **Then** my account is stored with owner onboarding intent.
- **Given** my account is configured for the owner portal, **When** I sign in after registration, **Then** I am routed to the restaurant onboarding or dashboard flow instead of the customer experience.
