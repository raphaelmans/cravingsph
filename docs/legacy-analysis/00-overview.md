# Legacy Analysis Overview

> What CravingsPH has today vs what the product needs. Audience: product team.

## What is CravingsPH?

A mobile-first platform that lets customers browse restaurant menus, customize orders, and (eventually) place orders — targeting the Philippine market.

## What Was Built

The legacy product delivered a **digital menu viewer**: customers visit a branch URL (e.g. via QR code), browse the menu by category, customize items with add-ons, and build a cart with a running total. No accounts, no ordering, no payments — purely a menu browsing and bill preview tool.

The new version added **user accounts** (login, registration, profiles) but has zero restaurant or menu functionality.

## Bottom Line

The legacy gave us the **customer experience** without the backend. The new version gave us the **backend** without the customer experience. Neither version lets a customer actually place an order.
